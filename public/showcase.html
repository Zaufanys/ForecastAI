Key Features

📤 CSV Upload

Import your forecast and actual sales in seconds. Data is saved locally and never sent to a server.

📈 Variance Analysis

Automatic KPI computation: forecast, actual, variance, accuracy, MAPE, bias, and rolling 3-month trends.

⚠️ Risk Scoring

Each dataset is graded Controlled / Watch / Critical based on accuracy and variance thresholds.

🎯 Driver Analysis

Top customer and product variance drivers, biggest single over- and under-runs highlighted.

🤖 AI Explainer

Deterministic narrative generator—every sentence maps to a computed metric, no model hallucination.

💾 Scenario Sensitivity

Stress-test under Base / Optimistic / Downside scenarios. See how KPIs shift under alternative outcomes.

📊 Live Filters

Filter by customer and product. Every metric, chart, and narrative recomputes instantly.

📥 Export Results

Download filtered data as CSV or generate a one-click Markdown executive summary.

What Makes It Different

Zero Dependencies. Zero Complexity.

  • No build step. Plain HTML, CSS, and ES modules. Run `npm start` and it goes.
  • No framework. Vanilla JavaScript. ~600 lines of pure, testable analytics core.
  • No server. Runs entirely in your browser. Data stays on your machine.
  • No account. No login, no cloud, no tracking. Upload, analyze, export.
  • No model dependency. The "AI explainer" is deterministic—metrics first, narrative second.
  • 24 unit tests. Full test coverage of the analytics core.
